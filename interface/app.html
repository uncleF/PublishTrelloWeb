Authorization

You'll have to authorize this application to get access to your Trello boards. When you do, it will be able to read all of your boards and teams. It won't be able to create or modify any of your cards, lists, boards or comments. It won't access your e-mail address or password.

Due to bug on Trello's side it's not possible to authorize application in Internet Explorer

Instructions

  1. Paste a link to a private or public Trello board you have access to.
  2. Decide in which formats you want to download it.
  3. Click Download Board.
